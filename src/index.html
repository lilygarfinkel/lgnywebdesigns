Social Media Isn't Enough

But.. I already have an established social media page. Do I really need a website too?

Websites and social media serve slightly different purposes within a brand's identity. Social media is great for promoting brand awareness, interacting with customers, and advertising new products. However, as your business grows with time, information can get buried in a page and questions from customers tend to get repeated.
This is where a website comes in. Your website will serve as a home-base for everything related to your business. You'll be able to direct customers to your site for any and all things related to your business- hours, contact information, your history, and whatever else you can think of.

Establish Professionalism

Consider your website as your brand's permanent online home. Social media is great for brand awareness and updates, but information can easily get buried with more posts over time. Your website should answer any and all questions a customer may have before interacting with you. A polished website tells customers that you're willing to take extra steps for your business to thrive.

Increased Security

It happens to the best of us; you finally hit your follower goal.. and then your account is hacked. Or, you become so popular that someone decides to impersonate your brand's accounts to scam your customers.
This is why a website is foundational to a business existing online. Because we code from scratch and don't use templates or plug-ins, there is basically nothing to hack.
